Blast Chillers and Freezers to Preserve Perishable Items

Blast Chillers and Freezers

Safe food preservation ensures that food wastage is reduced, bacterial growth is eliminated as well as the flavour, texture, integrity, and quality are retained for a longer shelf-life. This is where blast chillers and freezers play a critical role in solving the refrigeration needs of commercial kitchens as well as industries.

Neras, a leading choice in refrigeration solutions has precisely designed blast chillers and freezers with state-of-the-art technology to take extra care of your perishable items. We offer customised, modular models based on the capacity and the type of food item to be preserved.
