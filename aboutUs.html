Company Profile:

Established in the year 2016, NERAS TECH is known as a Turnkey Solutions Provider for Refrigeration & Cold Chain Industry. Our products are widely used in marine, Floriculture, Horticulture, Dairy, fruits and vegetables and pharma industry . We also undertake on-site installation & maintenance services for Air Conditioning Plants.

Expertise in Project Conceptualisation, Assisting Project Reports for Govt Subsidy Schemes. Design, Estimation &Planning, assisting customer in Scrutinising, Negotiations & Vendor Selections from India & Abroad, Project Supervision & Execution.

Our organization always adheres to principles of quality excellence, so that we can provide our clients a flawless range of products. We have partnered with hi-tech manufacturing plants, who has state of the art facilities to provide high quality materials which are needed to integrate your customised cold storage . It facilitates us in fabricating our products as per the client's requirements. A team of skilled professionals empowers us in designing and developing a qualitative assembly of products. Owing to these factors, we are able to ensure paramount client's satisfaction.

All of our business operations are managed and operated by
Mr. Prakash Padmanabhan, who has always been the major driving force behind our enormous success and growth. It is because of his sound business understanding, ethical business practices and management skills that we have acquired wide clientele across the domestic market.

Our team members have “hands-on” experience in setting up major infrastructure facilities like the state-of-art CA cold store cum pack-house facilities for fruits and vegetables, processing units, specialized pack-house for fruits and vegetables.
